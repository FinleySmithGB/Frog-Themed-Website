So you're feeling froggy!

Here is 3 fun facts about frogs!

  1. Frogs can catch prey with their tongues in under 1/10th of a second. That's pretty fast!

  2. They can breathe through their skin as well as their lungs.

  3. Frog eggs can hatch into tadpoles or young frogs, depending on the species.

    What it means to be a frog - Being a frog means embracing the art of adaptability and resilience. With the ability to thrive in both water and on land, frogs teach us how to navigate life's changes with ease. They embody the power of survival, having endured for millions of years, and their quiet stillness reminds us of the importance of reflection and peace in our busy lives. Frogs show us that true strength lies not just in action, but in finding balance and harmony with the world around us.
